🌱 AI-Powered Automated Plant Care System

Project Aim

  • To provide busy households with a self-sustaining, AI-powered automated irrigation system.
  • To ensure smart, eco-friendly, and hassle-free plant care without external electricity dependency.

Key Features

  • Self-Powered Electricity Generator: Works without external power (only initial jumpstart required), ensuring uninterrupted, sustainable operation.
  • Automated Irrigation with Rain Sensor: Detects rainfall and adjusts watering automatically to avoid over-irrigation.
  • Air Quality Index (AQI) Detection: Monitors local AQI and suggests suitable plants (e.g., Peace Lily, Aloe Vera for AQI 1–100).
  • Music for Plant Growth: Plays soothing meditation music to support healthier and faster plant development.
  • AI-Integrated Website & Application: Provides plant suggestions based on location, displays ideal humidity, includes an API, and has a user-friendly interface.

Overall Value

  • Combines automation, sustainability, and artificial intelligence.
  • Offers a hands-free, eco-conscious plant care solution.
  • Ideal for households, urban gardening, and smart homes.

Garden Guide

Watering 101

Let the top 2–3 cm of soil dry before watering most houseplants. Overwatering is the #1 cause of plant issues.

Light Basics

Bright, indirect light works for many indoor plants. South windows are strong—use sheer curtains to diffuse light.

Pot & Soil

Use a well-draining mix and a pot with a hole. Repot yearly to refresh nutrients and root space.

Pruning & Maintenance

Trim dead leaves and stems regularly to encourage new growth and maintain plant health.

Fertilizing

Feed plants with a balanced fertilizer every 2–4 weeks during growing season. Reduce feeding in winter months.

Pest Control

Check leaves for pests like aphids or mites. Use neem oil spray or organic insecticides when necessary.

Seasonal Care

Adjust watering and light exposure depending on the season. Protect plants indoors during harsh winters.
